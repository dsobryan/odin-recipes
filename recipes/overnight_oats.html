Overnight Oats

description

Overnight oats are oats that have been steeped in milk.

ingredients

  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup milk, dairy or dairy-free
  • 1/4 cup Greek yogurt, or dairy-free yogurt
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1 tbsp maple syrup

Steps

  1. Add ingredients into a sealable jar or bowl and mix to combine
  2. Let it soak in the fridge for at least 2 hours, but it's best to soak overnight for 8 hours. This will yield a creamier consistency.
  3. Top your overnight oats with your favourite toppings.
